HOW TO PAY

Step 1: Choose a Package Browse through our various packages and select the one that fits your requirements. You can view the details of each package on our "HiSa Packages" page.
Step 2: Fill Out the Package Form Complete the form with your details, including: Name, Email Address, Package Selected and Any Additional Requests or Requirements.
Submit the form, and our team will get in touch with you within 24 hours.
Step 3: We Work on Your Package Once we receive your form, our team will start working on creating your website based on the chosen package.
The process generally takes time depending on the package specifications.
Step 4: Receive a Screenshot Preview After the package is ready, we will send you a screenshot of your completed website. You can review the screenshot and request any minor changes if necessary.
Step 5: Payment for the Package Once you are satisfied with the preview, proceed with the payment. Payment methods accepted in Digital Wallets (PayPal or Google Pay).
Step 6: Receive the Final Website After the payment is confirmed, we will send you all the login details and files related to your website. You can then start editing, managing, or updating your website as needed.
    Additional Information:
  • Payment Security: All payments are processed securely through trusted payment gateways.
  • Customer Support: If you have any questions or issues during the process, our support team is available to assist you.
